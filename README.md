# **Sales & Profit Dashboard (Excel)**

## **Problem Statement**

The company has raw sales data but lacks a clear view of performance across **regions**, **products**, **customers**, and **states**.
A dashboard is needed to quickly analyze **sales**, **profit**, **orders**, and **product performance**.

---

## **Project Overview**

This project converts raw sales data into a clean and interactive **Excel Sales & Profit Dashboard**.
The workflow includes quick **data cleaning in Power Query**, summarizing insights using **Pivot Tables**, visualizing results with **Pivot Charts & KPI Cards**, and adding **Slicers** for easy filtering.
The dashboard provides a clear view of **key business metrics**, **regional performance**, **product profitability**, and **shipment trends**.

---

## **Dataset Summary**

* **Total Records:** 1952 orders
* **Includes:** Region, State, City, Segment, Category, Product Name, Sales, Quantity, Profit, Discount, Ship Mode, Order Dates
* **Purpose:** Analyze **sales & profit trends** across different business dimensions

---

## **Dashboard Highlights**

5. View **dashboard-preview.png** for a quick visual of the dashboard.
